CineBite

Year: 2025

CineBite UI/UX

Project Overview

CineBite is an app that allows you to order food directly to your seat in the movie theater, this way users don't have to leave their seat to wait in line for food, and instead they can enjoy the convenience of ordering the food directly to them. My job was to create an simple app UI/UX that was a total of 5 screens. These screens consisted of the home, menu, cart, checkout, and order status screens.
