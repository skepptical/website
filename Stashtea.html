Stash Tea Magazine Ad

Year: 2025

Magazine Ad

Project Overview

Stash Tea is a Portland, Oregon-based company that sells specialty teas. Stash Tea is known for their high-quality natural ingredients, and innovative tea blends. For this project, I wanted to create a magazine ad for their Tangerine Honey Daydream flavor.

Design Process

For the ad, I wanted to figure out a way to encorporate all the different elements of the Tangerine Honey Daydream into one attention grabbing photo that serves as an ad for this flavor. For the honey part, I created a tea bag hanging from a branch of a tangerine tree that looks like a beehive with dripping honey. I then added bees flying around as well. I also encorporated some tangerine flowers and slices blended into the beehive.
