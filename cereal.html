Coco Planets Cereal Box

Year: 2024

Project Overview

Coco Planets is a great-tasting chocolately cereal. Made with organic ingredients, Coco Planets is a delicious, but nutritious breakfast for you and your kids! For this project, I needed to create a package design for the front, back, and one side panel for the box.

Design Process

I wanted to create a eye catching design with a space theme and a simple brown color scheme. I also needed to create a fun and loveable mascot for the brand, so I decided to go with an astronaut cat which can be seen on the front panel. To fit with the space theme, I used the cats getting abducted on the side of the box, and on the back of the box is a maze in order to help free the cats from the aliens. The design of the box is intended to be fun and interactive for children.
