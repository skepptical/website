Green Glass Door Ads

Client:The Green Glass

Year: 2025

Project Overview

The Green Glass Door is a curated antique store that offers unique and quality items. The Green Glass Door caters to creatives and everyone who wants unique pieces to express themselves through the decoration of living spaces.

My goal was to create two different ads for the store in different formats. I chose to do a poster ad and a google ad.

Poster Ad

Google Ad

Design Process

The first thing I needed to decide was the format that I wanted to do for both ads. I decided to go with a poster ad and a google ad. I then had to decide what I wanted to advertise and went with a "25% off on all china dishes" sale.

For both ads, I had to stick with the pre-established fonts and colors of the brand. One issue that I ran into with this was that the main headline font didn't have number or symbols. On the poster, I worked around this by using the other font for the "25%" to bring contrast to it. for the google ad, I tried my best to create "25%" in the same font style to the best of my abilities. To incorporate the stained glass theme of the logo, I used similar shapes to create a path. I also incorprated the different graphic elements of the brand in the designs of the China dishes.
