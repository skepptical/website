Client:MRKT

Project Overview

MRKT is a music venue to open in the Haymarket District of Lincoln, Nebraska. The clients want the venue to be a modern, minimal, and stylish space that caters to a wide spectrum of music lovers. As well as being a music venue, MRKT also includes a bar with high-quality local beers and cocktails sa well as a gallery for local artists.
