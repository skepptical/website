Valentines Day Icon Set

Year: 2025

Project Overview

Cute valentines day icons designed not only for digital purposes, such as social media, embelishments, etc., but also for print material, such as stickers. This valentines icon set includes a love potion,coversation hearts, boquet of roses, a heart air balloon, a cake, teddybear, stamp, and a love letter.

Design Process

I first started by thinking about what theme I wanted to make my icons. I chose to make them Valentines Day related. I then needed to decide what objects I wanted. In order to figure this out, I made sketches of multiple different items and chose my favorite ones, after that, I built them out in Illustrator, making sure to stick with the same color palette for them so they had some sort of continuity besides the valentines theme.
