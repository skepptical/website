Spirited Away Movie Poater

Year: 2024

Project Overview

For this project, I had to create a movie poster. I chose to do Spirited Away. Spirited Away is a coming of age animated film written by Hayao Miyazaki, and animated by Studio Ghibli.

Design Process

For the movie poster, I wanted it to have a graffiti-esque aesthetic with a simple color palette of some of the bright colors that appear in the film.

I wanted to include two of the main characters. I found some stills from the film that I liked and cut the characters out from them. In order to make it fit better with the color of my poster, I played around with different filters until I got something that I liked.
