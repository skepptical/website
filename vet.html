Paws & Claws Veternarian Care Branding

Year: 2025

Logo

Project Overview

The Paws & Claws Veterinarian Care branding kit is designed to establish a warm, trustworthy, and professional visual identity for a veterinary clinic. I chose colors that I thought helped establish this identity.

Design Process

The firts thing that I needed to do was make a logo for the clinic. I first started by drawing many different sketches for potential logos.

I then picked three of my favorite and took them further.

Option 1

Option 2

Option 3

Brand Kit

I decided that I liked the third option the best, so that is the one that I used in order to create the main logo for the brand kit. Later on, I decided that I wanted to encorporate some of the elements of the other two designs in order to create the lockups and submarks.

Merchandise

Once I made the brand kit, I decided that I wanted to take it a little bit further and create a merchandise line for the brand. I made a uniform, a hat, clipboard, and a lanyard.
